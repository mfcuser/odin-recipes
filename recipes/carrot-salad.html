CARROT SALAD

This carrot salad is quick and easy to make with minimal ingredients.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pound shredded carrots
  • 1 ¼ cups raisins
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons mayonnaise, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

    • STEPS

    Step 1

    Mix shredded carrots and raisins together in a large serving bowl.

    Step 2

    Whisk mayonnaise, lemon juice, and salt in a small bowl until smooth. Pour over carrot mixture; stir until carrots and raisins are completely coated.

    Refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.
